Key hiring objectives for Innoventes :

        Short-term : 50 - 60 people by Dec 2025

        Long term: 500 people in five years.

 

Definition of a successful hire:

An individual with 2-3 years of industry experience.

        clears the internal and clients interview.

        accepts the offer.

        joins the organization on the specified date.

        stays in the organization for a minimum period of 24 months.

Current Talent Acquisition Process:

1.      Job requirements : Orally from the management / hiring manager; sometimes, a written JD from the client.

 

2.      The current talent acquisition process is as follows:

a)      Sourcing candidates : Naukri / agencies

b)     From Naukri, the TA team member downloads profiles of candidates that meet the clients JD.

c)      TA screens the profiles for a match.

d)     They invite the candidates to attend a proctored test on solutioning.

e)     The solutioning is evaluated by an internal team at Innoventes; if the technical solutioning is ok, the candidate goes through a technical round with the Innoventes tech team.

f)       Depending on the client, the candidate may have to attend a clients tech interview too.

g)      If there is no client interview / candidate clears the client interview then the candidate receives a formal offer:

h)     There could be 3 situations:

                 i.          Does not join: The candidate may not join because he or she may have got another offer.

                ii.          Demand for matching other offer(s): They come back with a demand for matching the remuneration or the terms of other offers that they may have.

               iii.          Onboarding. [SUCCESS]

3.      Employee referrals scheme exists but not active.

Cost of one current resource acquisition: CURRENT:

1. Sourcing through Naukri:

        1 TA resource cost : INR 30K (assuming 1 TA onboards 1 employee)

        Naukri platform charges INR 12 lakhs for 25K profiles. i.e. INR 48/profile.

        Cost of tech evaluation + inteview: assuming INR 2000/hr @ 3 hrs = INR 6000

        Cost of cumulative management time : assuming INR 10K/hr @ 5 hrs = INR 50000

Cost of hiring 1 candidate = 30,000+ 48 + 6,000 + 30,000 = INR 86,048 (@ time of offer letter)

Time : 20 - 35 business days

2. Sourcing through partners:

        1 TA resource cost : 2/3 of INR 30K (as TA does not use Naukri, liaises with partners)

        8.33% of the candidates yearly CTC approx. : INR 50,000

        Cost of tech evaluation + inteview: assuming INR 2000/hr @ 3 hrs = INR 6000

        Cost of cumulative management time : assuming INR 10K/hr @ 5 hrs = INR 50000

Cost of hiring 1 candidate = 20,000+ 50,000+ 6,000 + 30,000 = INR 126,000

Time : 15 - 30 business days

Main challenges:

       100:1 resume-to-hire ratio; 20 working days (~1 month) per hire

       Multiple interview rounds: screening, technical test, 2 technical interviews, HR round

        TA team's productivity currently stands at one TA having to work for one month to hire one candidate.

        The offer acceptance rate is 1%. i.e. if 100 profiles are downloaded from Naukri, 1 person joins.

       Customer interviews for specific roles add complexity

       Limited interview panel availability due to project commitments

       High candidate dropout rates, especially post-offer

       Over dependence on a single source (Naukri) for sourcing

       Lack of availability of internal technical team for solution validation and for interviews promptly. This introduces significant delay.

       Lack of brand awareness - TA team has to market Innoventes first and then convince the candidates to appear for technical capability screening.

       A small % where there is a disagreement about candidates quality between Innoventes and the client.

       A small % where the JD is unclear and a wrong profile is sent to the clients hiring manager.

       A small % where the skill sets are uncommon.

       Currently, it takes 45 days to fill a position.

Future SoP:

 

The entire recruitment process as Innoventes should move from being reactive to being responsive, and that will be the biggest transformation.

 

Continuous Sourcing

TA team should continuously nurture the employee referrals on an ongoing basis and keep profiles in their database. This internal database could also have the alumni of Innoventes along with the profiles of candidates who were earlier shortlisted and offered but they did not accept.

 

The TA team should continuously source candidates is by headhunting on LinkedIn, Indeed, and other job portals. They should also be a part of the HR network of India so that they are aware of the latest tools, technologies and techniques that are current in the industry.

 

Identify a list of agencies with whom they can collaborate in sharing profiles. Also, identify smaller IT companies from whom they can choose employees on a subcontracting basis.

 

The team ensures that there are multiple sources and each of these sources has to be ranked according to the quality of the candidates that they source and the job requirement.

 

Continuous empanelling of agencies and technical evaluators

The TA team can look at empanelling the various agencies and companies along with the technical evaluators who do this evaluation for a fee. This process should also be continuous.

 

Continuous networking

The TA team should participate in different industry seminars, webinars, liaison with other recruitment teams, and become a part of the different technical communities. Liaise with the engineering colleges' placement cell and the I.T training institutes who would have a database of qualified people. Doing so would expand their reach and also whenever there is a layoff they can actually get employees who are being soft placed from other organizations as well.

 

When the TA team receives a JD

 

Validation of JD:

When a job requirement is given, the TA team internally reviews that with their technical and internal technical mentor to ensure that they understand. If it is a client requirement, then a meeting is set up along with the internal technical mentor to understand the exact requirements and what the success criteria mean for the client. This has to be formalized with a quick and short email to the client.

 

Identify the list of keywords to be searched along with the list of requirements from the client, such as whether it is an on-site or a remote opportunity, or if there are any other specifications that the customer needs.

 

Prepare a very clear job definition including all of this so that it is clear to everybody in the TA team, and there are no ambiguities. You can use ChatGPT for this particular purpose or CoPilot, whichever is the organization standard.

 

Sourcing for the position:

Once a job requirement is confirmed, then the TA team needs to post it across their various sources based on the ranking.

Ranking could be as follows:

        Check if the internal database has such a profile.

        Next, post the requirement to the employees that there is an open job posting and solicit profiles from their friends and networks.

        Share it across the various sources.

 

Screening

Use internal tools or externally available AI tools to ensure that the source to profiles matches the keywords for the key requirements for the particular job profile.

 

Shortlisting

Once the candidate matches the profile matches the requirements. Then, the TA team reaches out to the prospect and does a preliminary communication skills screening.

 

If found okay, depending on the job requirements, either the candidate takes a proctored test or any other standardized skill test like Hacker Rank. Can this proctoring be outsourced / use of AI tools be used?

 

Technical Skills Evaluation

The candidate's solutioning is evaluated by an Innoventes technical architect or a technical solution person. If found to be ok, then the TA needs to schedule an interview either with an internal technical team or with the identified outsourced consultant.

This entire interview needs to be recorded with highlights. You can use an automated recording tool like Fathom or anything to identify what actually transpired during the interview.

 

Client interview

If the technical interview of the candidate is found to be okay, then the recommendation must be written by the Innoventes TA team as to why they find the candidate to be a match. They must identify all the various job requirements that have been formalized in the email, along with how they found this particular candidate to be okay. If there are any external proofs that have to be given, then that also needs to be included.

This can be generated using GPT or CoPilot.

The TA team has to follow up with the client to schedule the interview in the shortest possible time and keep the candidate engaged during this interval.

 

Making the offer

If the client makes an offer, if the candidate accepts the offer, then an offer is made. If the client doesn't make an offer, then depending on Innoventes' internal requirements, the candidate joins the internal pool of candidates.

 

Negotiation

If the candidate negotiates, this has to be probably documented and internally discussed. This is where the Exec team can come in and validate if it is a requirement, and exceptions can be made.

If a decision is made to let the candidate go, then this particular incident must be recorded as to why the candidate was allowed to let go, and the candidate's profile can be added into the shortlisted database for future references.

 

Even otherwise, if the candidate does not join, the TA team must reach out to the candidate and understand why the candidate has rejected their offer. This info would actually be used for further refining of the recruitment process.

 

Otherwise, if the exec team has agreed to go ahead with the offer to match the candidate's demands, then the new hire letter can be made.

 

The onboarding process can start. The TA team should continuously engage the candidate until they join the organization. A basic buddy system can be introduced so that when the candidate joins the client, they do not feel lost.
